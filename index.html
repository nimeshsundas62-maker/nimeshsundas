Background & Platform

Rooted in the Soil.
Driven by the People.

Nimesh Sundas has spent over eighteen years in public administration, but his true education comes from the tea gardens, flooded fields, and village roads of Matigara-Naxalbari. Having seen decades of broken promises and "vanishing" politicians, he is standing to break the cycle of neglect.

Unlike party candidates who answer to a high command in Kolkata or Delhi, Nimesh answers only to you. His platform is built on physical presence working in the mud and the fields alongside the community he represents.

Justice for Tea Gardens

Beyond slogans, we will enforce timely wage payments and build secondary schools within walking distance of the gardens.

Infrastructure & Safety

Building roads that don't wash away and repairing Mechi embankments through community-led accountability.

Dignity For All

Fighting for the constitutional rights of the Rajbanshi and Tribal communities, including legal land titles for the undocumented.
